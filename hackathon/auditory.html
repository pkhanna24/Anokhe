White Noise

White noise contains all frequencies equally and sounds like static or a fan. Some of the benefits of white noise are that it can improve sleep, reduce crying in babies, improve your work performance, and lower ADHD symptoms.

Pink Noise

Pink noise has more energy in low-frequency ranges than high ones and sounds like rainfall or traffic. Some of the benefits of pink noise are that it can filter out distracting noises and create a more soothing sound for sleep.

Click Link

Brown Noise

Brown noise has more energy in lower frequencies and sounds like a deep roar of an ocean wave or thunder. Some of the benefits of brown noise are that it can help lower symptoms of ringing in your ears and improve your thinking skills.
